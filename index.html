Roasted Cauliflower

Description

This cauliflower recipe is very simple and only need two ingreidants to make this tasty dish.

Ingrediants

  • 1-2 head of Cauliflower
  • olive oil
  • salt (I suggest Truffle salt)

Steps

  1. Cut the cauliflower head into medium chinks
  2. Put in in a steel tray
  3. Pre-head the oven to roast at 425f
  4. Add the olive oil evenly with the cauliflower
  5. Add the salt to the califlower
  6. Put in the oven
  7. Set a timer for 35-40 Minutes
  8. Take out when the califlore is golden or dark brown

Roasted Zucchini

Description

This zuchini recipe is so simple and so tastly that it make you feel like your a real chef.

Ingrediants

  • 1-3 pieces of Zucchini
  • Olive oil
  • Salt (I suggest Truffle salt)
  • Sauce brush

Steps

  1. Cut the Zucchini into medium-thin slices
  2. Put in in a steel tray
  3. Pre-head the oven to roast at 425f
  4. Add the olive oil on the Zucchini evenly
  5. Use the Brush to help spead out the Olive oil onto the Zucchini
  6. Add the salt to the Zucchini(make sure you don't over seaon it)
  7. Put in the oven
  8. Set a timer for 35-40 Minutes/li>
  9. take out when the Zuchini is since and brown
