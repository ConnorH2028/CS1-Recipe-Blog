Roasted Cauliflower

Description

This recipe is very simple and only need two ingreidants to make this tasty dish.

Ingrediants

  • 1-2 head of Cauliflower
  • olive oil
  • salt (I suggest Truffle salt)

Steps

  1. Cut the cauliflower head into medium chinks
  2. Put in in a steel tray
  3. Pre-head the oven to roast at 425f
  4. Add the olive oil evenly with the cauliflower
